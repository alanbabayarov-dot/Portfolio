TOOLOR × World Nomad Games

fashion designer · 2025–2026 · official national team collection · Kyrgyzstan

Official apparel for the VI World Nomad Games 2026 — Kyrgyzstan's national team collection. The brief: carry 3,000 years of nomadic visual culture into athletic function without sentimentality. Petroglyphs as side panels. Topographic contour lines as ornament. The ibex as emblem. Shot on-location against the Cholpon-Ata rock carvings — the same stones that sourced the graphic language of the collection.

FILE 05 — TOOLOR
05

TOOLOR — fashion system

founder · fashion designer · 2024 → ongoing · outerwear / brand identity

TOOLOR is built on the premise that Central Asian design heritage is not decorative — it is structural. The brand's signature outerwear piece: a stand-collar overcoat in technical shell. Minimal exterior — one badge, one silk label, a line of runic inscription at the back yoke. Interior lined entirely in a field of ancient Orkhon script. What you see from the street is restraint. What you see from the inside is the archive. The garment as private statement.

003 — Studio · how I work

One person.
Three disciplines.
One bloodline.

Alan Babayarov is a Fashion Designer, Creative Director and AI Creative Specialist based in Central Asia. He builds concept-driven work where nomadic heritage meets generative technology — fashion that carries cultural memory, brands that speak in a singular voice, AI systems that make creative work faster without making it thinner. Bishkek-born. Briefed worldwide. The studio is an atelier of one: him, the archive, and the machine.

— 01

Fashion design

Concept-driven garments and collections rooted in Central Asian nomadic culture, sacred geometry and contemporary form. Each piece is a proposition, not just a product.

— 02

Creative direction

Brand strategy and visual identity for fashion brands and cultural initiatives. Visual systems that hold — identity, language and image locked to a singular character.

— 03

AI creative systems

Custom AI workflows for fashion design, brand development and visual storytelling. Architecture that accelerates intuition. One engagement per quarter — deep, not wide.
