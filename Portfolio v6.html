003 — Manifesto · what this is

Directed for
the podium.
Engineered for
the feed.

I lead a design team. Creative direction here is not a single idea — it is a built pipeline: a national uniform, a metaverse collection and a mass-market drop move through one system without dilution. AI is the connective tissue that lets a team of this size hold state-level precision, digital-native production and commercial volume at once. Born in Moscow, April 2000. Inside LVMH certified, 2025.

— 01

State-level work

A uniform for the Olympics or the World Nomad Games has one shot: a stadium, a broadcast, a technical committee that checks every seam before it checks the story.

— 02

Metaverse / digital

A collection built in CLO3D and Unreal Engine 5 has no fabric at all — its only test is whether the render holds up under a camera that never existed until someone built it.

— 03

Mass market

A casual piece for the mass market has the least glamorous test of all: does a stranger buy it. Three different juries, three definitions of "good" — one pipeline that survives all of them.
