About / manifesto

Alan Babayarov · /visualkan
01 //

I am a designer between worlds.

Born in Moscow, working from Bishkek. Fashion, 3D and AI — same practice, three doors. /visualkan is the codename. EULER is the lab.
02 //

Build fashion in our own language.

Central Asia is not a costume to borrow. It is a grammar. The future deserves to be written in it, not translated from it.
03 //

Treat code as cloth.

3D, AI and computational craft are not gimmicks. They are looms. I weave with both.
04 //

The garment is the artifact. The story is the asset.

Every piece carries a narrative platform: ritual, geometry, lineage. Identity is not optional.
